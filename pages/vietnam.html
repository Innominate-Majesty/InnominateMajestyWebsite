Vietnam
Photo Credit: Trip Adviser Co.

Vietnam is a Southeast Asian country, located south of China. It is famous for its scenic beaches, rivers, pagodas, and vibrant cities. The capital of Vietnam is Hanoi, located in the north of the country. The country has individuals from diverse backgrounds, with many food and culture to enjoy and share when visiting.
