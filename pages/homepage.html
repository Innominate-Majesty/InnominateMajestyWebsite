About Me

Welcome to my page! I’m Venus. I’m currently a student studying computer science, biological science/neuroscience, hoping to enter the engineering field in the future. I came to the United States in 2010 with my parents from a small village called Ma Am in Lam Dong Province in Vietnam. Our village mostly consists of individuals who are of the Churu (or Chu Ru) ethnic group. We have a total population of 23,242 (2019) around the world. My parents were farmers in Vietnam and came here hoping to provide my siblings and I better opportunities and future. I hope to take advantage of this opportunity and create a better future for myself while inspiring young children and others to do the same! Check out more about me below!
