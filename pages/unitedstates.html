Photo Credit: Japji Travel

The United States of America, or simply United States, is a country located in the North America continent. It is known for its variety of culture, people, and diverse landscapes. Its capital is Washington District of Columbia, located in the east coast of the country. There are a total of 50 states and 16 territories.
